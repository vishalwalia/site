Library -

Presently 1000 books are in the library, 5 newspapers, 2 magazine, journals. Library needs special grants for text books and encyclopaedia.

I.T. Infrastructure -

Under the RUSA grant sanctioned, the college has created a language/I.T. lab with 10 computers. For internet connectivity, the college has acquired V-Set. College has 4 printers.

Maintenance of campus facilities -

In order to maintain the campus a railing was constructed in 2015. For the maintenance of existing campus an amount sufficient is given to PWD. All the students are aware of maintaininf the campus clean. Cleanliness drives are also organised by NSS and Eco-Club.
